# Wave Mage Game Document Design (GDD)
Github wave 11/2025 Game Jam

# General Description
## Title
Wave Mage
## Genre
Roguelike, Strategy and Dungeon Crawler
## Audience
Not needed Right now
## Concept
A mage that generates waves, the waves interact with structures placed by the player and finally
The waves hit the enemies dealing damage, the structures amplify the damage of the waves in various ways

# Playeability and Mechanics
## Objectives
Kill your enemies and survive their attacks
## Controls
Mouse and click
## Progression
When buying powers the character gets stronger and also going inside the dungeon there are
Harder enemies.
## Mechanics
1. Buying the correct powers
2. Placing the structures correctly on the map

# History and Worldbuilding
## Plot
You are a wave mage and you actually are really weak, so weak that there is no wave magic that 
actually makes damage to enemies, because your frustration you created the damaging sound wave,
 but it deals 1 hp. Angry you enter a dungeon to prove other mages that a wave mage could be as
 powerful as any other mage, there you find artefacts/structures to boost your power and to 
 actually defeat your enemies
